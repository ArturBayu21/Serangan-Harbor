Serangan Island, Denpasar

Turtle Island

location is very strategic and easily accessible to everyone. We are located on Serangan Island, South Denpasar. Serangan Island is a village that is famous for its tourism objects, such as turtle conservation, underwater tourism, and culinary, no wonder this island has earned the nickname turtle island. Apart from being a tourism area, Serangan Island is also famous for its harbor, which is not only used to park boats, but also as a vacation spot.

With a thick culture, beautiful nature, Serangan Island won't disappoint you.
